Balu Pass

One of the easier trips in Rogers Pass, with a long valley walk and good ski run at the end. The ski run is consistent and open, generally 22 degrees.

  • Length: 10km return
  • Elevation Gain: 730m
  • Aspect: South / East
  • Best Season: December-March
